Beheer Afsprakenstelsel eToegang : LinkedDeclarationSignatureValue

Created by Sander Boer, last modified by Unknown User (liannedevries) on Sep 09, 2020
DescriptionThe value of the signature of the referenced Assertion.

Name

urn:etoegang:core:LinkedDeclarationSignatureValue

Typehttp://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#base64Binary
Issuer (XACML)Applicable in case attribute is enclosed in XACML: EntityID of the MR issuing the linked declaration.
Value(copy of the) Base64 value of the SignatureValue of the Signature by the Issuer of the referenced Assertion.
Comment

Including the SignatureValue links the containing declaration to the referenced Assertion. Neither Assertion can be altered without breaking the Signatures, providing cryptographic assurance of the reference from the containing Assertion to the referenced declaration.
